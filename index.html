All You Can Play

By: Deepika Koripelli & Manasvi Bhavaraju

Directions:

Dino Game - Click the space bar to jump and avoid the cacti!

Ping Pong Game - Player one can use the arrow keys to move their paddle and player two can us WASD to move their paddle. Keep the ball in play for as long as you can!

Tic-Tac-Toe Game - This is a two player tic tact toe game. Try to get three in a row to win!
